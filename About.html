About Nicholas Schumacher

Home About Hobbies

Nick, a 28-year-old ICU nurse originally from Northern California, has been living in San Diego for the past 5 years. Growing up, sports played a significant role in his life as he dedicated much of his time to baseball and basketball. His love for sports continued into college, where he played Division 2 baseball at Azusa Pacific University.

After graduating with a degree, Nick embarked on an alternative career path and enrolled in an accelerated Bachelors of Science in Nursing program. Since then, he has been a dedicated ICU nurse for the past 2.5 years, finding his work to be incredibly rewarding though undeniably challenging at times.

In addition to his professional pursuits, Nick enjoys spending time with family, with roots and a love of camping, fishing, and hiking. An advocate for the great outdoors, Nick has ventured to various outdoor destinations, from Yosemite to Santa Cruz, where he has created countless memories and found solace in the beauty of nature.

Amidst his outdoor adventures, Nick has fostered a great love for animals, having had a black lab named Levi and even a chameleon named Loki. He also possesses a remarkable green thumb, evident in his passion for gardening, housing a multitude of 70 plants at one point.

Beyond his outdoor and animal interests, Nick unwinds by engaging in other hobbies, from playing video games and enjoying nature walks with his friends, to discovering new culinary experiences and unique coffee shops. A devoted fan of music, Nick has a diverse taste and even had the pleasure of attending a Coldplay concert last October.

With his wealth of diverse experiences and passions, Nick hopes to share some of these cherished memories through the captivating photos that have influenced and enriched his life.
